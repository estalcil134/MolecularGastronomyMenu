魚の影

Sakana no Kage | 一期一会

A singular moment of taste

始まり — Hajimari

Checkerboard Sashimi Rose
Scallop and salmon fused into a delicate rose with uni-chili oil, a floral evocation of oceanic harmony.

森の香り — Mori no Kaori

Earth Mushroom Soil
Curry-spiced chocolate soil with roasted root vegetables and pearl onions, a grounding introduction to nature’s umami.

熱 — Netsu

Hedgehog Pork Belly
Crisp pork belly with Thai foam and garlic herb mayo, echoing street market fire and indulgence.

転換 — Tenkan

Artichoke Chicken Roulade
Cheese, spinach, and artichoke chicken roll, softly changing the palate for sweetness to come.

余韻 — Yoin

Beef Shigureni with Yuzu
Slow-simmered shigureni beef, yuzu reduction, and bonito rice. An echo of sweetness flows through the grain.

安堵 — Ando

Quattro Cheese Bread
Molten cheese bread served with chili oil aioli and pesto—comfort at the table’s turn.

終わり — Owari

Coconut Mousse 'Fried Egg'
Tropical coconut mousse, mango-passion sphere, sesame tuile and milk bread. A playful close with vibrant drink.
